How the integration works

YoppyChat's Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture acts as a seamless bridge between your content and your community. First, our platform will securely connect to YouTube Channel to extract spoken knowledge from your videos. This creates a dedicated "Brain" for your agent without having to manually transcribe or organize your content.

Next, you will generate a one-click deployment for Discord. Whenever a user asks a question, the agent searches your YouTube Channel database, retrieves the exact factual answer, and formats it naturally for Discord to answer technical questions and moderate sentiment in real-time. No hallucinations, no manual flowcharts, just automated knowledge transfer.

Step-by-Step Setup

  3. Set your bot's personality: Define the tone and rules for your new digital agent.
