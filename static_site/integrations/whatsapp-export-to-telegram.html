How the integration works

YoppyChat's Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture acts as a seamless bridge between your content and your community. First, our platform will securely connect to WhatsApp Chat Export to ingest your historical .txt chat logs to learn your exact brand tone and sales framing. This creates a dedicated "Brain" for your agent replicating the success of your best human sales representatives.

Next, you will generate a one-click deployment for Telegram. Whenever a user asks a question, the agent searches your WhatsApp Chat Export database, retrieves the exact factual answer, and formats it naturally for Telegram to provide instant value and announcements in your chat. No hallucinations, no manual flowcharts, just automated knowledge transfer.
