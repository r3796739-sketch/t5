Why Do You Need a Google Review QR Code?

Asking customers for reviews verbally is awkward and forgettable. Sending a WhatsApp review link is better — but having a scannable Google business review QR code at your counter is the most powerful method because:

  • The ask happens at peak happiness — right after a great meal or service
  • No staff training needed — the poster works 24/7 silently
  • Works offline — no internet needed to display the QR code poster
  • AI suggestions on the review page help customers write the perfect 5-star review in seconds

How to Create a Google Review QR Code — Step by Step

Step 1: Find Your Google Business Profile

Go to YoppyChat's Google Reviews dashboard. In the search field, type your exact Google Business name — e.g. "Sharma Sweet House, Connaught Place" or "Priya Beauty Salon, Koramangala".

YoppyChat uses the Google Places API to locate your listing and extract your unique review link automatically. No Google account login needed on your end.

💡 Pro tip: If your business name is common, add your city or neighbourhood to find the right listing faster.

Step 2: Generate Your QR Code & Review Link

Click Generate QR Code. Two things are instantly created:

  • AA scannable Google review QR code that opens your review page on any Android or iPhone camera app
  • BA short review link you can copy and paste into WhatsApp, SMS, or email

Choose your poster style: Google Style (official branding), YoppyChat Orange (most popular with Indian shops), or Minimal Dark (premium brands & hotels).

Step 3: Download, Print & Display

Click Download Poster to save a high-resolution PNG. Print it at any local print shop (A4 or A5 size works best) or at home. Laminate it for durability.

Where to display your Google review QR code poster:

  • Payment counter / billing desk — catches customers at the highest-satisfaction moment
  • Table tops in restaurants — customers have time to scan while waiting for the bill
  • Reception desk in clinics & salons — visible while customers wait to leave
  • Exit door / sticker on the back of the door — last thing customers see
  • Packaging & delivery bags — for e-commerce and cloud kitchens delivering across India

🎯 Bonus tip for India: Also share the review link via WhatsApp to customers within 2 hours of service. This combination of in-store QR code + WhatsApp review link follow-up typically 3x review volume within the first month.
