What Are Cookies?

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status, making your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for several purposes, including:

Authentication & Security

We use cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session securely. These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of our authentication system.

Preferences & Settings

Cookies help us remember your preferences, such as language settings and display options, so you don't have to set them every time you visit.

Analytics & Performance

We use cookies to understand how you interact with our Service, which helps us improve functionality and user experience. This includes tracking page views, session duration, and feature usage.

Service Functionality

Essential cookies enable core functionality such as page navigation, access to secure areas, and maintaining your chat history during your session.
