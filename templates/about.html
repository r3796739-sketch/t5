About YoppyChat AI

Bridging the gap between creators and their communities through conversation.

Our Mission

Every YouTube creator builds a universe of knowledge, stories, and personality through their videos. But as an audience grows, the ability for personal interaction shrinks. Questions get lost in comments, and valuable insights remain buried in hours of content. Our mission is to unlock that universe. We believe every fan should be able to instantly tap into a creator's wisdom, and every creator should be able to engage their community at scale, even when they're offline.

The Problem We Solve

Creators are experts, entertainers, and teachers. Their viewers are curious, engaged, and eager to learn more. The comment section is a chaotic place for deep interaction, and finding a specific piece of information can mean scrubbing through dozens of videos. This creates a bottleneck, limiting the depth of engagement and leaving the community's curiosity unfulfilled.

Our Solution

YoppyChat transforms a creator's entire video library into a smart, conversational AI that embodies their unique persona. It's not just a search engine; it's a digital extension of the creator. By training on every transcript, the AI learns their tone, opinions, and expertise. This allows fans to ask complex questions and receive nuanced answers that sound authentic, complete with source links to the exact moments in the videos.
